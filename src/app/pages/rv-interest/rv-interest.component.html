What are Followed Interests?

A followed interest subscribes you to content you want to see in your feed.

You can select a few interest now or wait until after you log in by selecting the My Followed Interest menu item under your user profile icon.

You can add, remove or change Followed Interest at any time. Changes to followed interest will immediately update your feed

You can start customizing your feed by clicking on any combination of categories to the right, then press add. You can create multiple Followed Interest this way.

What are Followed Interests?

Followed Interests subscribe you to the Mods you want to see in your feed. Customize your feed by filling out any combination of data fields and then pressing “Add”. Your feed will then display all Mods that meet any of the Followed Interests that you’ve set-up.

Examples: If you want to see all storage related mods (regardless of RV class, make, series, model, or year), you would need to add one Followed Interest:

Mod Category = “Storage”

If you want to see all 2019 and 2020 Winnebago Class C Navion (all models/categories, but only for those selected years), you would need to add 2 separate Followed Interests:

  • RV Class = “Class C”, RV Make = “Winnebago”, RV Series = “Navion” and Year = “2019”
  • RV Class = “Class C”, RV Make = “Winnebago”, RV Series = “Navion” and Year = “2020”

Curate your feed by selecting any combination of RV and Mods that you are interested in following.
