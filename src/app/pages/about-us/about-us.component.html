Our Mission

To make it easy for RV enthusiasts to discover and share mods, experiences, connect and learn

Dave & Cheri Holdan

How It Started

Born from Dave and Cheri Holdan’s frustration while researching mods for their RV across disorganized online forums and groups. Clear, consistent information and instructions were rarely available.

They wished there was one place they could go to learn about the interesting ways others modded their RVs.

Dave got to work building a team to design and begin developing the RVmodshare platform, but lacked the knowledge that comes from years of hands on modding and social media experience.

Dave knew he needed advisors who could guide the team on developing the most valuable features and functionality that a modder would need.

Dave pitched the idea to the Internet’s most renowned modding couple, James and Stef Adinaro from The FitRV www.thefitrv.com. They loved the idea and have been helping to shape the vision of the platform.
